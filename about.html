What we are doing

We Are In A Mission To Help The Helpless

The Mission

Therestella's Foundation is dedicated to improving the well-being of individuals and communities in Ghana by advocating for maternal and child health, empowering youth, supporting the elderly, providing public health education, and conducting free health screenings and outreaches.

The Vision

Our vision is to create a society where every person, regardless of age or background, experiences improved health and well-being. We aspire to bring smiles to the faces of individuals through targeted advocacy, education, and healthcare initiatives, fostering a healthier and more resilient community.

The Passion

Our journey transcends traditional boundaries, guided by the fervor that comes from a shared commitment to public health. We believe that true change is born out of passion, and it is this shared enthusiasm that propels us to address the most pressing health challenges of our time..

About our foundetion

We Are In A Mission To Help Helpless

Therestella's Foundation was born from a deeply personal tragedy when the the founder's ( me)baby went missing during her cesarean section, leading to profound psychological distress. Fueled by this experience, I have dedicated myself to supporting postnatal mothers facing mental health crises.

Driven by a love for sharing knowledge, I embarked on a mission to educate communities on various health topics. Being a Philanthropist from a young age where back in school I supported friends and loved ones in various forms, I have channelled this act of kindness and love into actions, bringing smiles to the underserved through community outreach, health education, and donations.

This compassionate journey is dedicated to the loving memory of my late father, Mr. Joseph Clifford Obosu, ( who was a great Philanthropist and former deputy director of DVLA). My story is a testament to transforming personal pain into a positive force for change, embodying resilience, kindness, and a commitment to the well-being of others.
